What Is Reserve Now ?

You lost your train back home ?

Are you stuck in a new city and you don't know anybody ?

Don't panic! You are not alone. Reserve Now is a place for those who need some help but rending an airbnb is too expensive. With Reserve Now you can find people can help for real, not only get some cash out of your pocket.

My Story

I experience many moments waiting for a train too late or just walking on the street in a new city just because I didn't have where to stay for few hours.

So I wanted to build a platform where people can support this people like me.

How It works ?

  • Create an account(we also support Google Authentication for fast registration)
  • Pick a city where you want spend your night or just few hours
  • Find an announce that fit your needs or post one yourself
  • Chat with someone or wait until someone can help you

What Is The Price ?

Using the platform costs you no money.

I encourage helping the others for no money because this is an emergency. Anyway if you can offer any services to your host in a way would be great. What this means ?

If your host need an electrition and you are an electrition and can help you can change your skills for few hours of hosting. I like to call this win-win!
